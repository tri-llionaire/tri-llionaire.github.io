adverbs:
formed from adjectives:
first two declensions: drop -s, -a, -um and add -e
third declension: drop -is and add -iter

comparative adjectives:
drop ending, add -ior for masc/fem and -ius for neuter

superlative adjectives:
drop ending, add -issimus, -issima, or -issimum
